First F-Droid release.

• Offline password manager — no network, no accounts, no telemetry
• Argon2id KDF, AES-256-GCM per-field encryption, Keystore-wrapped keys
• Master password, 6-digit PIN, biometric unlock
• System Autofill Service with URL-only matching
• Built-in TOTP / 2FA codes alongside credentials
• Import from Google, LastPass, KeePass, 1Password, Dashlane, NordPass, Safari
• Encrypted .1key backup + plain CSV / JSON export
• Reproducible builds under GPL-3.0-or-later
